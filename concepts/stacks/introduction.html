Operations in Stack

  • Push
  • Pop

Usage of Stack

  • Undo in text editors
  • Compiler Syntax checking for braces and paranthesis
  • to model a pile of books and plates or tower of hanoi.
  • Used behind the scenes to support recursion by keeping track of previous function calls.
  • Depth first search for Graph

Complexity for the operations in Stack

Pushing O(1)
Popping O(1)
Peeking O(1)
Searching O(n)
Size O(1)
